uu.trac: trac ticket listing integration add-on for Plone

Features:
---------

 * Listing tickets.

 * A content proxy for each ticket.

 * Ability to assign priorities to tickets in areas defined on the listing
   container/folder.

 * Ability to get an effort score based on estimated hours and difficulty.

 * Ability to obtain a priority listing of items based on ratio of
   effort to priority score.  "Low hanging fruit" view.
